Services

Lead Work

Lead work is my speciality.
Lead is the most recycled and recovered building material in use today. It will last longer and age more beautifully than any of its synthetically produced substitutes.

Slating

Ideal for a broad range of roofing applications, natural, fibre cement and interlocking slates can contribute considerably to a building’s whole life costs.

Tiling

With different factors impacting on the look, design and performance of a tile roof covering, including planning restrictions, weather conditions or aesthetics, talk to a roofing contractor that cares about meeting all your needs in a finish that looks the part.

Flat Roofing

Although not a standard element of British architecture because of our climate, flat roofing is fast becoming one of the most popular solutions – especially for commercial and industrial buildings – due to the many systems and materials now available.
